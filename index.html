How to Apply for Universities & Scholarships

Follow our 5-step process to successfully secure your place at a university or funding for your studies.

1

Research & Choose

Identify your preferred universities and scholarships that match your profile and career goals.

2

Prepare Documents

Gather academic transcripts, recommendation letters, personal statements, and identification documents.

3

Craft Your Application

Write compelling essays and tailor your application to highlight your strengths and alignment with the opportunity.

4

Submit & Track

Submit your application before the deadline and keep track of all correspondence and portal updates.

5

Prepare for Interviews

If shortlisted, prepare thoroughly for potential interviews or additional assessments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer scholarships? How much do your mentorship services cost? +

Ms Mentorship does not offer any financial aid nor are we a university agent. Our Mentorship services are free of charge. However, assignment writing and private tutoring services are not free.

Can you guarantee I will get a scholarship or admission? +

While we cannot guarantee outcomes, we significantly increase your chances. We provide expert advice, review your applications, and teach you strategies that have helped hundreds of students succeed. Your dedication is also key.

What are the most common requirements for international scholarships? +
  • Excellent academic transcripts (minimum B+ average or equivalent).
  • Proof of English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL) for non-English speaking countries.
  • A compelling personal statement or motivation letter.
  • Letters of recommendation from teachers or professors.
  • A valid passport and often a medical certificate.
How early should I start preparing for university applications either in Zambia or abroad? +

Considering the ECZ calender, We recommend startingas soon as you finish writing your exams but before intended intake. For universities abroad, you might even need to start while in school. This gives you time to research, prepare for any required entrance exams (SAT, ACT, etc.), write compelling essays, and gather all necessary documents without rushing.

Do you help with visa applications? +

No, at the moment, we do not assist with any visa applications.
